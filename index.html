Overview

Our objective here at KH RIA is to develop innovative processes that will allow us to assist our clients who are seeking to register their investment advisory firm.

Our team consists of former regulators and industry experts that have the experience to provide your firm the necessary guidance in this important first step of registration of your firm. We have the knowledge and background that will ensure your firm’s operations are compliant with regulatory agencies.

At KH RIA, we specialize and assist our clients with registration in the jurisdiction in which they operate. Our services are curtailed to meet your individual needs. We stay current with the constant changes that occur in the industry and make it a priority to stay informed with those changes. Whether it be new legislation that has passed or streamlined operations that will impact our clients in the investment related industry, we have the solutions and will ensure we deliver it consistently to service the needs of our clients. We promise to provide unparalleled service to each and every one of our clients.

Services

Our team here at KH Squared RIA of former regulators and industry experts will assist your firm in the process of registration in the jurisdiction that you will operate. We will guide your firm with the initial planning of your business and ensure that your firm’s registration process is seamless as possible so that you can focus your attention on other areas of your business. Our understanding of regulatory and compliance laws within the industry will allow us to work diligently without compromising efficiency. Included with our service, we will provide your firm with all of the required documents needed to be compliant with in jurisdiction that you operate.
